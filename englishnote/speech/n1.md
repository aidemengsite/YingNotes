## 第一季
### 第一集
Don't you wanna go down?
> go down(in history) 载入史册；传递下去

> `Those guys build a mediocre piece of software, that might be worth something someday, and no they live here.`
1. mediocre 平庸的；普通的；adj.
2. a piece of software   一款软件。一般不直接说a software
3. worth 值得的；有价值的。adj. 价值；意义；n.

> `There's money flying all over silicon valley, but none of it ever seems to hit us.`
1. hit someone. 这里不是打；而是指落在某人头上（机会、运气）。
The inspiration finnally hit me.    灵感终于降临了。
2. all over 到处都是 adj.   ever 总是;曾经;     
I'll never ever do that again.  我再也不会干那种事了
3. seem to 似乎；看起来 seem 似乎；好像可能；v.
